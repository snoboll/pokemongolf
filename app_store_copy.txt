DESCRIPTION (4,000 chars max)
------------------------------
Bogeybeasts turns every round of golf into a monster-catching adventure.

After each hole, a random Bogeybeast appears on the course — and your golf score determines your odds of catching it. Drain an eagle? Near-guaranteed catch. Rack up triple bogeys? Good luck with that Legendary.

HOW IT WORKS
• Start a round — pick from 23 real courses in Skåne, Sweden, or add your own.
• Play each hole — log your score: eagle, birdie, par, bogey, and beyond.
• Encounter a Bogeybeast — a creature appears, weighted by rarity.
• Catch attempt — your score drives the catch rate. Play well, catch more.
• Build your Bogeydex — track all 151 Bogeybeasts across every round.

RARITY TIERS
Five tiers from Common to Legendary, each with distinct catch rates. Legendary Bogeybeasts are rare to encounter and even harder to catch — consecutive pars build a streak that raises your legendary odds.

TERRAIN MODIFIERS
Toggle one-putt, bunker, water, or rough to boost encounter rates for matching Bogeybeast types. Play the course, not just the scorecard.

BOGEYDEX
Browse all 151 Gen 1 Bogeybeasts with caught/uncaught filters and rarity badges. Your collection syncs across devices.

COURSES & LEADERBOARD
Choose from Örestads GK, Falsterbo GK, Barsebäck Resort, PGA National Sweden, and 19 more — or create a custom course. See how your Bogeydex stacks up against other golfers on the leaderboard.

CLOUD SYNC
Sign in once and your collection, rounds, and scorecard history follow you everywhere.

Whether you're a casual weekend golfer or chasing that elusive Legendary, Bogeybeasts gives every hole a reason to care.
